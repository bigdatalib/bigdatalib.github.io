Data Science for Trading and Risk Management

GARP YYC Data Science Lab is a Hybrid Real/Virtual event that will cover topics related to Data Science and Trading/Market Analytics/Risk Management. The intent of these meetings are to create a virtual environment for the participants to continue their discussions even after the live meeting is over.

These meetings are organized by GARP Calgary

We are planning number of Real Time Session in Calender 2015/2016

  • Introduction to R for Risk Managers and Trading Professionals
  • Creating Interactive Online Data Products
  • Basics of Statistical Inference and Machine Learning
  • Data Science on Cloud - Introduction to Microsoft Azure ML
  • Data Science Scale Up - Introduction to Hadoop/Spark
  • Data Visulization - D3 and R
